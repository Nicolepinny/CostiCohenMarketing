SEO position and priorities · 15 March 2026

Core finding: Costi Cohen has strong brand equity and a well-designed website, but is being held back by a low-impact SEO engagement — poor-quality link building, limited content volume, and a lack of digital PR. The single highest-leverage investment is redirecting budget from low-quality link building into digital PR and content production.

Current position vs Rethink
Organic traffic
1x vs ~5x
Rethink commands approximately five times the organic traffic of Costi Cohen.
Keywords
~2,000 behind
Rethink has approximately 2,000 more indexed keywords across guides, calculators and content.
Bright spots
Calculators working
Property Investment Calculator ranking #9. Stamp duty calculator already generating traffic after one month.
OMG engagement concerns
Low-quality link building
Placement sites (A-Z Business Finder, LinkCenter, Find Us Here) receive little organic traffic. Google can identify and devalue these networks.
Risk
DR reporting discrepancies
Two sites reported as DR 72 and DR 48 are actually DR 18 and DR 8 in Ahrefs. Significant reporting concern.
Risk
Over-optimised anchor text
Commercial property keywords used across multiple placements — unnatural link signature, genuine penalty risk.
Risk
Estimated spend since inception
$5,000/month since March 2023 — in excess of $60,000 spent. Do not continue paying for this type of link building.
Strategic priorities
Priority 1 — Digital PR
Engage digital PR specialist at ~$1,250/day, 2 days/month ($2,500/month). Commission one-off research survey ($3,000–$4,000) for 10–15 data points powering 6 months of press releases. Target AFR, Money Magazine, SmartCompany, Yahoo Finance.
Highest leverage
Priority 2 — Calculator hub
Add commercial stamp duty, rental yield, cash-on-cash return, and mortgage repayment tools. Create parent hub page for internal link equity.
Priority 3 — Content gaps
10 high-priority pages Rethink ranks for that Costi Cohen doesn't: commercial due diligence checklist, lease types, valuation methods, SMSF investing, commercial vs residential, state guides (NSW, QLD, VIC).
Priority 4 — Case study pages
Transform portfolio listings into narrative case studies (800–1,200 words each) with client brief, alternatives considered, agent rationale, outcome. Include agent name, photo, and client quote.
Priority 5 — Commercial property podcast
Replicates Rethink's 10+ podcast platform backlinks. ~$400–500/episode in studio. Clips for social. Guest network builds compounding reach.
Priority 6 — Upgrade existing content
Audit underperforming pages. Pull top-ranking competitor pages. Close gaps with data tables, diagrams, FAQs, expanded sections. AI tooling accelerates this.
